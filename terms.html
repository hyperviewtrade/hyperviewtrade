02Description of Service

Hyperview is a mobile trading terminal designed for the Hyperliquid decentralized exchange, available on iOS. The App provides the following features and capabilities:

  • Advanced Charting — Professional-grade charting powered by TradingView, including multiple chart types, drawing tools, technical indicators, and timeframe analysis.
  • Real-Time Market Data — Live price feeds, order book data, funding rates, and market statistics sourced directly from the Hyperliquid exchange.
  • Wallet Tracking and Analytics — Comprehensive wallet analysis including open positions, trade history, portfolio composition, PnL breakdown, win rate, and performance metrics for any Hyperliquid address.
  • Position Monitoring — Real-time tracking of open positions with entry price, unrealized PnL, liquidation distance, margin health, and funding rate exposure.
  • Copy Trading Features — The ability to follow and copy trades from top-performing traders on the Hyperliquid leaderboard with customizable size multipliers.
  • Liquidation Heatmaps — Visual representations of liquidation levels across markets to identify areas of concentrated risk.
  • LARP Detector — On-chain verification tool that cross-references claimed positions with actual blockchain data to identify potentially misleading trading claims.
  • Native HYPE Staking — Built-in support for staking HYPE tokens, including validator selection, reward monitoring, and staking management.

Hyperview connects to the Hyperliquid decentralized exchange and blockchain infrastructure. The App serves as a front-end interface and does not operate its own exchange, custody user funds, or process transactions independently of the underlying blockchain network.
