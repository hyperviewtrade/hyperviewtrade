Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Hyperview ("we," "our," or "us") is developed and operated by Alexandre Camugliani, based in France. We operate the Hyperview mobile application (the "App"), an iOS trading terminal for the Hyperliquid decentralized exchange.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By accessing or using Hyperview, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not access or use the App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. As a service provider based in France, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable European data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Wallet Address: When you connect your wallet to the App, we collect your Hyperliquid wallet address. This is necessary to display your positions, execute trades, and provide core App functionality.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us for support, we may collect your name and email address as provided by you in your correspondence.
  • User Preferences: Settings and preferences you configure within the App, such as display options, watchlists, and notification preferences.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Analytics: Information about how you interact with the App, including features used, screens viewed, session duration, and interaction patterns.
  • Crash Reports and Performance Data: Diagnostic data including crash logs, performance metrics, and error reports to help us identify and resolve technical issues.
  • Log Data: When you use the App, our servers may automatically record information including your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, timestamps, and referring/exit pages.

2.3 Blockchain Data

We access publicly available blockchain data from the Hyperliquid L1 network, including but not limited to:

  • Open and historical trading positions
  • Transaction history and trade executions
  • Wallet balances and token holdings
  • Staking positions and rewards
  • Leaderboard rankings and performance metrics

This data is inherently public by the nature of blockchain technology. Anyone with access to the Hyperliquid network can view this information. We aggregate and display this data to provide you with a better trading experience.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we never collect, store, or have access to:

  • Private keys — We never request, collect, or store your private keys.
  • Seed phrases or recovery phrases — We have no access to your wallet seed phrase.
  • Passwords — We do not use password-based authentication.
  • Personal identification documents — We do not perform KYC or collect identity documents.
  • Financial information beyond on-chain data — We do not collect bank account numbers, credit card information, or any off-chain financial data.
  • Biometric data — We do not collect fingerprint, face, or other biometric information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing Core Functionality: To operate, maintain, and deliver the features of the App, including displaying your trading positions, portfolio data, and market information.
  • Trade Execution: To submit your trade orders to the Hyperliquid blockchain through authenticated API calls.
  • Portfolio Display: To display your current positions, profit and loss, wallet balances, and staking information.
  • Improving the App: To understand usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and improve App performance, stability, and user experience.
  • Service Notifications: To send important service-related communications, such as updates about the App, security alerts, or changes to this Privacy Policy.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot problems, and provide technical assistance.
  • Technical Issue Prevention: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, bugs, and potential security vulnerabilities.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract Performance (Article 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to provide you with the App's services, including displaying positions and executing trades.
  • Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)): Processing necessary for our legitimate interests, such as improving the App, ensuring security, and preventing fraud, where such interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): Where we rely on your consent for specific processing activities, such as sending optional notifications. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c)): Processing necessary to comply with legal obligations to which we are subject.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with or relies on the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing the data they collect:

5.1 TradingView

We use TradingView's Charting Library to provide advanced charting functionality within the App. TradingView may collect usage data related to chart interactions. TradingView's privacy policy is available at tradingview.com/privacy-policy.

5.2 Hyperliquid

We interact with the Hyperliquid blockchain and its APIs to fetch market data, account information, and execute transactions on your behalf. All interactions with Hyperliquid are governed by the decentralized nature of the protocol. Transaction data submitted to the blockchain is publicly visible and immutable.

5.3 Apple

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect data related to your download and usage of the App in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy, available at apple.com/legal/privacy. We may receive anonymized and aggregated analytics data from Apple regarding App usage.

5.4 Analytics

We may use anonymized analytics services to understand how users interact with the App and to improve its performance and usability. Any analytics data collected is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users. We do not sell or share analytics data with third parties for advertising purposes.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your wallet connection data, preferences, and cached information are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by iOS (Keychain and encrypted local storage).
  • No Private Key Storage: We do not store your private keys or seed phrases — not on your device through our App, not on our servers, not anywhere. Wallet authentication is handled through secure signing mechanisms.
  • Real-Time Data: Market data, position information, and portfolio data are fetched in real time from the Hyperliquid blockchain and are not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we process, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), secure API communication, and regular security assessments.
  • Data Minimization: We follow the principle of data minimization, collecting only the data strictly necessary to provide our services.

7. Data Retention

  • Locally Stored Data: Data stored on your device persists until you delete the App or clear the App's data. You have full control over this data.
  • Server-Side Data: Any data processed on our servers (such as anonymized analytics or crash reports) is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, and no longer than 24 months.
  • Support Correspondence: Communications related to support requests are retained for up to 12 months after the resolution of your inquiry, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
  • Data Deletion: You may request deletion of any personal data we hold about you at any time by contacting us at Hyperview@proton.me. We will process your request within 30 days in accordance with GDPR requirements.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Blockchain Transactions: When you execute trades or other on-chain actions through the App, transaction data is submitted to the Hyperliquid blockchain, which is publicly accessible.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the App, provided they agree to keep your information confidential and comply with applicable data protection laws.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, or situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a data subject under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access (Article 15): You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, along with information about how it is processed.
  • Right to Rectification (Article 16): You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure (Article 17): You have the right to request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18): You have the right to request that we limit the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability (Article 20): You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and to transmit that data to another controller.
  • Right to Object (Article 21): You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data based on our legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In France, the relevant authority is the Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés (CNIL) — cnil.fr.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at Hyperview@proton.me. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Hyperview is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at Hyperview@proton.me, and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Cryptocurrency trading involves significant financial risk and is not appropriate for minors. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a user under the age of 18, we will take immediate steps to delete that data and terminate the associated account access.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily processed in France and within the European Union. In cases where data may be transferred to countries outside the EU/EEA, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission for the recipient country
  • Other lawful transfer mechanisms under GDPR Chapter V

We will never transfer your personal data to a jurisdiction that does not provide adequate protection without implementing appropriate safeguards.

12. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

As a native iOS application, Hyperview does not use browser cookies. However, we may use similar local storage technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Storage: To store your wallet connection status, user preferences, and session information necessary for the App to function.
  • Analytics: To collect anonymized usage data that helps us improve the App.

We do not use tracking technologies for advertising or cross-app tracking purposes. Hyperview respects the iOS App Tracking Transparency framework and does not track users across other apps or websites.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Post the revised Privacy Policy on this page
  • Where appropriate, notify you through the App or via other communication channels

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data. Your continued use of the App after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
