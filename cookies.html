How I use cookies on my website

Last updated: March 18, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

2. How I Use Cookies

My website (hyperviewapp.github.io) uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics cookies: I may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use my website

I do NOT use:

  • Advertising cookies
  • Tracking cookies
  • Third-party marketing cookies

3. The Hyperview App

The Hyperview iOS application does not use cookies. It stores minimal local data on your device for app functionality (such as your preferences and wallet connection).

4. Third-Party Cookies

My website may load resources from:

  • Google Fonts — font delivery
  • GitHub — repository links

These services may set their own cookies per their respective privacy policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

6. Changes

I may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, please contact me at contact@hyperviewtrade.com.
